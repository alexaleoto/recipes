Pan Galactic Gargle Blaster

"The Best Drink in Existance"

Ingredients

  • One bottle of Ol'Janx Spirit.
  • One measure of water from the seas of Santraginus V.
  • Three cubes of Arcturan mega-gin
  • Four liters of Fallian marsh gas.
  • A measure of Qalactin hypermint extract.
  • The tooth of an Algolian suntiger.
  • A sprinkle of Zamphour.
  • One olive.
