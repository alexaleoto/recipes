Hot Pockets

"Zillions of Scrumptious Flavours"

Perfect for on the go!

How to Prepare

Oven Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°ƒ
  2. Unwrap sanwich and place on baking sheet.
  3. Bake for 28 minutes, then carefully remove from oven.
  4. Let cool, and enjoy

Microwave Directions

  1. Unwrap sandwhich, insert into crisping sleeve,and place on microwave safe plate
  2. Microwave for two minutes
  3. Let sit for two minutes, enjoy!
